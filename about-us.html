Welcome to CostadeGranada.es, your online store specializing in the sale of native and exotic plants from the beautiful Granada coast. Our mission is to bring the beauty and diversity of coastal flora to every corner of your home and garden.

We are a team of nature and environment enthusiasts, with a deep admiration for the biodiversity of our region. We founded CostadeGranada with the aim of promoting the use of local plants and contributing to the conservation of the coastal ecosystem.

We aspire to be the reference point for all plant lovers on the Granada coast. We want to inspire our customers to create green spaces that not only beautify their homes but also promote sustainability and respect for our natural environment.

At CostadeGranada, you'll find a wide selection of carefully cultivated and selected plants that are adapted to the region's climatic conditions. We offer:

  • Native Plants: Perfect for sustainable and low-maintenance gardens.
  • Exotic Plants: To add a touch of color and originality to your spaces.

We are committed to quality and customer satisfaction. All of our plants are cultivated using responsible and sustainable methods. Additionally, we offer personalized advice to help you choose the most suitable plants for your needs and garden environment.
